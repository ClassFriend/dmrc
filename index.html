Welcome To

Dr. Mahbubur Rahman Mollah College (DMRC)

Dr. Mahbubur Rahman Mollah, the famous Principal of one of the best educational institutions in Bangladesh named Shamsul Hoque Khan School & College, established Dr. Mahbubur Rahman Mollah College (DMRC) after his own name in 2010 with the co-operation of all concerned amidst high expectation of the honorable guardians and the students to ensure standard and modern education. The college which started its journey with only 261 students has now become one of the best colleges in the country ensuring quality education and excellent result with the far sighted guidance of its founder Chairman Dr. Mahbubur Rahman Mollah and the creative and great works of the Principal Md. Obaidullah Nayan and the sincere work of the young, experienced teachers and above all the integrated approach of the students, teachers and guardians.
